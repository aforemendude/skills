### High — Findings can copy secrets into the generated report

- Location: `plugins/agent-review/skills/review-markdown/SKILL.md:66`,
  `plugins/agent-review/skills/review-markdown/SKILL.md:111-113`
- Problematic location: The prompt-review checklist includes secrets, while the finding format requests the “problematic
  text or location” without any redaction rule.
- Problem: A reviewer that discovers a credential, token, private key, or other sensitive value has no instruction to
  avoid quoting that value in the report. The requested finding format makes reproducing the raw text a plausible
  implementation.
- Impact: The skill can create an additional persistent copy of a secret in a report that may later be shared or
  committed.
- Recommendation: Add an output-safety rule that raw secrets and sensitive personal data must never be reproduced.
  Require redaction plus a file-and-line reference, and recommend revocation or rotation when an exposed credential is
  found.
